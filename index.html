NATURAL STAR NANI


Biography

Ghanta Naveen Babu (born 24 February 1984), known professionally by his screen name Nani, is an Indian actor, film producer and television presenter who predominantly works in Telugu cinema. Nani is a recipient of several awards including two Nandi Awards, three Filmfare Awards South and three South Indian International Movie Awards.
